Armours

Mark I

Information

The Mark I Armor was Tony Stark's first Iron Man suit, built to help him escape from forced captivity by the Ten Rings.

Armor Composition

The armor's pieces are composed of a crude metal alloy. A leather suit is worn under the heavy armor by Tony.

Strength and Capabilities

1. User-Strength: capable of throwing powerful punches or kicks at enemies.
2. Increased Protection: User is fully protected in vital areas against dangerous threats such as gunfire and minor explosions.

Armoury

1. Rocket: The armor is equipped with a powerful single shot rocket, which is attached to the arm piece of the armor.
2. Flamethrower: The armor has flamethrowers mounted on the right arm. The device is fueled by small gas tanks behind it.

Systems

The Mark I has no special or notable systems, aside from the basic programming, internal systems and power the armor needed for movement and use of weapons.

Executional Drawback

Suit was build of scarp misile material. so it had single shot missiles and one time leg jet booster fuel with limited flight capabilities.

Mark II

Information

The Mark II armor was designed and created by Tony Stark after his return from Afghanistan. The main focus of the Mark II was to update the armor with advanced construction and material with a focus to greatly enhancing it's flight capabilities.

Armor Composition

It was constructed as a prototype with an emphasis on exploring flight potential.

Strength and Capabilities

1. Flaps: The Mark II has steel flaps which are used for breaking air speed when flying or traveling in the air.
2. Stabilizers: The Mark II has ailerons and flaps built into the legs and shoulders to increase flight stability.

Armoury

1. Repulsors: The Mark II has standard repulsors, which were used mainly for flight power and stability.
2. Unibeam: The Mark II has a standard Unibeam, and has a whitish blue glow coming from the Unibeam's power source. The Mark II is powered by the Palladium Arc Reactor Mark II.

Systems

*JARVIS OS: JARVIS is integrated into the armor, allowing him to control the suit's systems, manage the rest of the armor's functionalities, and assist Tony when using the armor.

Executional Drawback

While testing the flight mechanism, he noted that 'icing' would become a major problem with the Mark II when upper altitudes were reached.

Mark III

Information

The Mark III (Mark 3) was the third Iron Man Armor designed and created by Tony Stark and is the successor to the Mark II. The armor was designed with upgraded technology and improved features to surpass its predecessor's capabilities. It is also the classic armor.

Armor Composition

The armor is composed of a 95.5% Titanium and 4.5% Gold Alloy with ceramic plating and silicon infused-steel.

Strength and Capabilities

1. Ice Resistance: Mark III was given an advanced upgrade which enabled it to resist the build up of ice when flying at high altitudes.
2. Super-Strength: The Mark III is capable of amplifying the user's strength to extreme levels, allowing the wearer to lift very heavy objects, such as cars.

Armoury

1. Shoulder-Mounted Guns: The armor is equipped with six-barrel miniguns which are mounted on its shoulders for quick use and is supported by the suit's Targeting System.which allows it to have superb and perfect precision and accuracy when fired at its target.
2. Kinetic Micro-Missiles: The Mark III is equipped with a Kinetic Micro-Missiles on each arm, capable of penetrating at least 400mm LoS.
3. Flares: The Mark III is equipped with flares, which are used for emergency purposes or to confuse Heat Seeking Missile's targeting system.

Systems

*J.A.R.V.I.S. OS : JARVIS is integrated into the armor, allowing him to assist Tony and manage the armor's condition as well as the systems and functions of the Mark III.

Executional Drawback

Had No Drawback As an In View of that time of technology.

Mark V

Information

Emergency Suit as Fits as a Suit-Case.
The suit was built as a base for experimenting on newer and more advanced transport features for Tony's suits, so as he would be able to don them faster than before.

Armor Composition

The Mark V is made out of an unknown metal that is not the gold-titanium alloy of the other Iron Man platforms, and is composed of inter-weaving bands of small plates, instead of interlocking large plates, lowering defenses, but allowing it to be collapsed into a much smaller size, roughly the size of a briefcase, when not in use.

Strength and Capabilities

The underlying arm mechanics and motors are extended with it and wrap around his arms while the armor plates slide into place to be deployed. The leg and waist armor plates slide down and begins wrapping around the underneath assembly.

Armoury

1. The Mark V is only armed with a pair of standard hand-mounted repulsors. Their relative strength versus the other more advanced armors is unknown, but they manage to serve as reliable weapons in an unexpected attack.
2. The Mark V has the standard Unibeam, but is not as powerful as the other armors because of the energy output it has. It is powered by the new Palladium Arc Reactor Mark III.

Mark VI

Information

The Mark VI (Mark 6) is an All-Purpose Suit, and was the sixth suit built and created by Tony Stark after he found the solution to the Palladium core's poisoning, which was the newly improved Arc Reactor.

Armor Composition

The Mark VI's armor design is based on the Mark IV, with the exception of the new triangular shaped Unibeam on its Chest Piece and armor coloring.

Strength and Capabilities

1. Water Resistance : The Mark VI was the first suit built by Tony Stark that could be fully submerged into the water and still function properly, as seen in the opening of The Avengers.
2. Electric Resistance : The Mark VI has shown itself capable of weathering Thor's lightning with only superficial damage and going toe-to-toe with the Thunder God himself. Even absorbing the energy from Thor's lightning and supercharging the power of the suit to 475%.

Armoury

2. Mini-Missiles

4. heat-seeking missiles
